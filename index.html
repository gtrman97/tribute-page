Vinnie Moore

Above: Vinnie Moore, playing live with his signature Vinman Electric Guitar by Dean Guitars in 2011.

Born to Shred

  • 1964 - Born in New Castle, Delaware
  • 1984 - Discovered by Mike Varney
  • 1985 - Appeared in a Pepsi commercial
  • 1986 - Released his first solo album "Mind's Eye" receivng awards from guitar magazines and selling over 100,000 copies
  • 1988 - Released his second solo album "Time Odyssey"
  • 1991 - Joined Alice Cooper's band and appeared on the "Hey Stoopid" album and released his third solo album "Meltdown"
  • 1996 - Released his fourth solo album "Out of Nowhere"
  • 1999 - Released his fifth solo album "The Maze"
  • 2001 - Released his sixth solo album "Defying Gravity"
  • 2003 - Joins the rock band UFO as the lead guitarist

"I always write, so I have more tunes than I can put on a record." - Vinnie Moore
